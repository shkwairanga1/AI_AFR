SECTION 1: INCLUSION CRITERIA
----------------------------------------------------------------
A paper must meet ALL of the following to be included:

IC1. Published in a peer-reviewed journal (no conference
     abstracts, preprints, or grey literature)

IC2. Study involves neuroscience OR a neurological condition
     (brain, spinal cord, peripheral nervous system,
     psychiatric conditions with neurological basis)

IC3. Uses Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep
     Learning, or Neural Networks as a primary or major
     component of the methodology

IC4. Has a direct Nigeria connection — defined as AT LEAST
     ONE of the following:
       (a) Lead/first author is Nigeria-based (Nigerian
           institution affiliation)
       (b) Corresponding author is Nigeria-based
       (c) Study data/patient population is from Nigeria

IC5. Full abstract available in PubMed

IC6. Written in English

IC7. Published between 2000 and 2026 (to capture the AI era)


----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: EXCLUSION CRITERIA
----------------------------------------------------------------
A paper must be EXCLUDED if it meets ANY of the following:

EC1. No Nigerian author affiliation AND no Nigerian data
     (e.g. purely international study that merely mentions
     Nigeria in passing)

EC2. AI is mentioned only in the background/introduction
     but NOT used in the study methodology

EC3. Duplicate record (same study published twice —
     keep the more complete version)

EC4. Conference abstract only — no full peer-reviewed paper

EC5. Preprint not yet peer-reviewed (e.g. bioRxiv, medRxiv)
     unless subsequently published

EC6. Animal studies only (no human participants or human
     clinical data)

EC7. No neurological relevance — purely cardiovascular,
     oncology, or other non-neuro even if AI is used
     EXCEPTION: keep if brain/CNS involvement is explicit
     (e.g. brain tumour, neuro-oncology)

EC8. Study is purely statistical/epidemiological with no
     AI component (regression alone does not count as AI
     unless framed as ML)

EC9. Full text completely unavailable and abstract
     insufficient to extract key data
